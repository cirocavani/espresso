MongoDB Server:

	wget http://fastdl.mongodb.org/linux/mongodb-linux-x86_64-2.4.5.tgz
	tar xzf mongodb-linux-x86_64-2.4.5.tgz
	cd mongodb-linux-x86_64-2.4.5
	mkdir data
	bin/mongod --dbpath data
